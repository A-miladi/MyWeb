web development cost
The cost of web development typically varies based on the complexity of the project and the technologies used. A custom-coded website developed from scratch by a front-end developer costs around $800 on average. This price reflects the time and expertise required to build a site with specific designs, features, and functionalities tailored to the client’s needs. Web development usually involves building websites accessible on browsers, whether for businesses, blogs, or online platforms. In contrast, app development—which costs around $1000—focuses on creating software applications for mobile devices, often requiring more intricate work to ensure compatibility across different platforms like iOS and Android.
the segmentation of the costs

One major difference in pricing and approach is between custom-coded websites and platforms like WordPress. A custom-coded site is built from the ground up, offering full flexibility, scalability, and control over design and features, making it ideal for businesses with specific needs. In contrast, WordPress and similar content management systems (CMS) allow for faster and cheaper development, relying on pre-made templates and plugins, which makes them more affordable but less customizable. Apps developed using pre-built tools or platforms like WordPress for websites generally offer limited customization, whereas custom coding offers more flexibility and unique solutions, but at a higher cost.
